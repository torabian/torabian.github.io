# Meshora Opentherm Smart Gateway

Smart OpenTherm compatible gateway for smart heating. Capable of connecting to boilers, heatpumps
which supporting this feature. Easy installation, and comes in different screen sizes, makes it
super easy for installators to give the modern astetics to rooms.

<img src="/meshora-opentherm/meshora-opentherm.png" style={{'width': '400px'}} />

Features:

- Supports connecting to your wifi router.
- Supports hotspot mode.
- Smart compensation for outside temperature
- Room temperature sensor
- Touch screen
- Small design

Price range: **70-120**€

## Viessman Vitodens compatible.

Very easy installation with Vitodens boilers, series such as 50W and 100W.

## Compatible with smart valves up to 12

You can control with single device valves up to 12, to separate the room
temperature, with additional Meshora Room Control.
